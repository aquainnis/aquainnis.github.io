How we work

We never bite off more than we can handle.

Every engagement is scoped to around three days of focused work — small enough to finish, big enough to matter. Larger goals get broken into pieces that each stand on their own.

01

Understand

We start with the actual problem — not a discovery phase, a conversation. What needs to be true when we're done.

02

Design

The simplest solution that holds up. Fewer moving parts, nothing you'll maintain that you didn't ask for.

03

Build

Built and documented as we go, so the finished thing is clear enough for you to own without us.

You get a working solution and the understanding to run it. No lock-in, no dependency on a retainer.
