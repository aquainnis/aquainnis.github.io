The Problem

Most companies on AWS are overspending — without realising it.

Idle resources, oversized instances, and Savings Plans that no longer match usage quietly stack up every month. It rarely shows up on anyone’s radar — because nobody owns it. I’m Luke, founder of Aquainnis. I spent seven years as a Solutions Architect at AWS, and I now help companies find and cut that waste.
