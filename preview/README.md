## Why Aquainnis

In a world of overpriced, overcomplicated IT, we keep things simple. We take on
intentionally small, scoped pieces of work that actually get finished — with
senior expertise, no bloated scopes, and no sales theatre. Founded in 2021 by a
former AWS Solutions Architect with 20+ years in cloud, data, and identity.
